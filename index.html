Preschool

Vivriti (P) Ltd, founded in 2008, was running the Serra Preschool franchisee from June 2011 to March 2016. Vivriti has decided to launch our own preschool in the academic year 2016-17. The school will be managed by Vivriti Edu Management (P)Ltd, which was running Serra Preschool franchisee for the last five years. There is no change in the management, operating hours or location of the preschool.
The preschool is situated in a 5000 sq.ft house with calm surroundings. The pre-school has been setup with world-class facilities and child friendly interiors and exteriors that offer a warm, secure and stimulating environment for the children. The preschool houses five multi-purpose rooms, separate parents lounge and open spaces of over 2000 sq ft. There is an open classroom in the second floor, which is designed for creative and physical activities such as Yoga, Dance etc. The facility meets all the developmental needs of the preschool age children. Key attractions of the school include a sand pit, child-friendly splash pool, an Atelier (art studio), nature trail/organic garden cared for by the children. It is a cosmopolitan set up with children from different nationalities.
The preschool team @ Vivriti consists of a highly committed passionate educators who believe in providing high quality early childhood education & experience. With the help of our advisory panel and a team of experts we have developed an appropriate curriculum to meet the global standards. Our staff is well trained to support our students by creating exciting and rewarding learning environment to help the little ones develop holistically.

  • highly-skilled staff,
  • small class sizes and high adult-to-child ratios,
  • a language-rich environment,
  • age-appropriate curricula and
  • stimulating materials in a safe physical setting,
  • warm, responsive interactions between staff and children
We believe in an approach that “lets children be children”, which means learning through play and experiencing a wide range of artistic, cultural, cognitive, social and physical activities

After School Enrtichment program

The After-school Program is intended to enrich the child’s experience during the hours after school through activities structured thoughtfully to meet the developmental needs of our children. Our idea is to provide a combination of academic, cultural, fitness and recreational activities in a project-based or theme-based manner. The activities are designed to broaden children’s world, to take them beyond the core curriculum and further stimulate their minds, enhance their development and help them explore the many opportunities that await them. We also design certain activities to tap into the natural play and inquisitiveness of our children. The programs are designed for children between the ages of 4 to 12 years, These are some of the activities planned for the children

1. Scientific Inquiry time:
The facilitators create an environment in which children explore to acquire basic science inquiry skills (predicting, observing, explaining) and to transfer these skills to age appropriate writing, schematic representation or painting. By deepening thoughts through experiential learning and discovery, a child progresses from knowledge acquisition to analysis and application. We go beyond "what" and examine "why" and "how."
2. Technology time:
We believe that general technological competencies are a must for young children in a rapidly emerging hi-tech society. We introduce the children to internet and related technical skills. We have always believed that technology can have a positive impact on students, as an educational tool, to enhance learning, increase productivity and promote creativity.
3. Fitness time:
Helps keep your child active and healthy; daily Yoga, dance movements with Anitha .
4. Recreational activities:
Dance classes by dance teacher Swapna on Wednesdays and Fridays. Music by Pragnya on tuesdays
5. Soft-Skill development:
Emphasis on developing leadership and communication skills. We encourage the children to speak about their topic of interest onstage with their peers and teachers as audience
6. Nature walks, Park visits & Outdoor play:
These activities support the enhancement of the natural curiosity of the child along with the physical developmental needs.
7. Arts &Craft Time
